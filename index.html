About us

ABOUT US

We aim to help the incoming class of 9th & 10th graders get adjusted to high school and get them needed help from the 11th and 12th graders in an informal friendly way.

With us, lowerclassmen, or newbies, have an easier experience in picking classes, time management, SAT & PSAT prep, volunteering, forming connections, and finding tutoring opportunities with the help of upperclassmen, or buddies.
